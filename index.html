What is TAMID?

TAMID is a fast growing business organization that fosters professional development by connecting students to the Israeli economy. Founded at the University of Michigan in 2008, TAMID is thriving at over twenty campuses including Harvard, Berkeley, and Penn. Each chapter runs its own educational program, student-managed investment fund, and a consulting practice with real Israeli startups. Additionally, TAMID runs an exclusive summer fellowship program in Tel-Aviv where members can intern at startups or companies like Google and Deloitte.
